Other projects

    Besides endeavors targeted towards its core goals in education, the Door of Awareness team also runs various ancillary ventures.

Projects

Charity School Startup and Incubation Facility

Our Charity School Incubation facility is built on our vision of reaching out to as many marginalized communities as possible. We provide incubation and start-up assistance to individuals or corporations who wish to set up free schools to give back to the community. Our team is able to provide extensive operational, academic and on-ground support to deliver quality education and vocational skills.

Free Teacher Coaching

Our talented academic team provides free training sessions to teachers and school leaders of underprivileged schools. The training covers areas such as teaching methodologies, classroom management, and educational leadership. Teachers and academics can avail these training sessions at the Door of Awareness Head Office in Model Town by contacting us and booking a slot.

Thrift Shop

Why not donate belongings that are no longer used? With this objective, Door of Awareness has set up thrift shops within its premises in order to keep such items of use in circulation. Such donated items can range from clothes to household appliances and are sold at a very nominal price within underprivileged communities. This method of keeping commodities in circulation gives our beneficiaries access to affordable goods and a sense of ownership.

Matriculation Project

The Matriculation Project is focused on enrolling underprivileged students who have either failed or dropped out during their Matriculation program. Door of Awareness conducts specialized focused classes for such students to pass their exams and gain access to sustainable employment opportunities.

Free Medical Camps and Dispensaries

Door of Awareness organizes ad hoc free medical camps for its students and their parents. These camps focus on vaccinations, updating medical records, medical guidance and provision of basic medicines.
