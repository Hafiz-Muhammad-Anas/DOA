Female Empowerment

Female empowerment is at the core of our work. The Door of Awareness Female Empowerment division provides vocational training to women from deprived communities. This training, such as dress-making, IT courses, and stitching & sewing empower these women to work and earn an independent livelihood for themselves and their families.

650 women from underprivileged communities

5 stitching centers operating in Lahore

3 to 6 month courses range

Free Training covers fundamental knowledge and skills for dress design and tailoring

Stitching Courses

Our stitching centers are equipped with manual sewing machines as well as all the essential professional equipment. Our vision is to link our trainees with employers and guide them in setting up their micro-enterprise thereby enabling them to start the journey towards attaining social mobility.

Adult Literacy Courses

We also offer Adult Literacy Courses for illiterate women, which enable them to become productive and proactive members of society. Having an education allows them to earn for their families and increase their civic engagement.

Female Discussion Forums

Besides these courses, we regularly conduct Female Discussion Forums within our schools and training centers. These discussions are conducted by volunteers and aim to address issues of women from local communities around our schools. This platform facilitates discussion amongst underprivileged and often illiterate women regarding their concerns, which include learning about their rights, health, hygiene, and finding solutions to the issues they face.

IT Courses

The IT Training division at Door of Awareness focuses on girls and women with limited exposure and skills in IT. We seek to equip them with the necessary knowledge as well as skills that enhance their employability and competitiveness in the job market. Our courses offer a thorough introduction to Windows OS and cover the essentials of various professional software such as Microsoft Office, Coreldraw, and Adobe. The trainees learn these vital skills crucial to securing competitive jobs in the corporate world or start off their own freelancing work.

About Us

Established in 2007, Door of Awareness (DOA) is a not-for-profit charitable organization set up to aid and educate the underprivileged students of rural and urban areas of Pakistan.
