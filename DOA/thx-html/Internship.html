The Internship Program at Door of Awareness offers a great opportunity for individuals seeking to make a positive difference in their community as well as gain valuable work experience.

The program was started in 2010
Interns have spent more than 15,000 hours with Door of Awareness
Interns can also be part of the Door of Awareness Youth Wing after their internships
All interns receive a Certificate of Participation at the end of their work
Interns get involved in a range of fun activities all year round
Interns are able to make a real, sustainable impact by working directly with students and teachers

SUMMER INTERNSHIP PROGRAM

Our most popular program, the Summer Internship Program runs from June through August every year. The internships are customizable and range between 2 to 8 weeks. We welcome students from all schools, colleges, and universities to intern with us.

Our Internships can be varied, and volunteers can conduct activities such as

Teaching and coaching students
Conducting fun activities with students
Decorating and renovating schools
Leading art classes
Conducting co-curricular activities with the schools
Preparing plays, skits, and dramas with the students
Organizing charity drives
Developing marketing material
Teaching our students new skills
Conducting photography sessions in schools
Organizing sports tournaments
Conducting library and book reading sessions for students
