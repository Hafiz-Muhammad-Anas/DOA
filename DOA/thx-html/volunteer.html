Volunteer

Are you keen to get involved and contribute to society at large?

There are plenty of ways to get involved with Door of Awareness and work for sustainable social change.

Intern With Us

To find out more about the Door of Awareness Internship Program, visit the Internship Page.

internship

Get involve with DOA

Fundraise for Us

All the great work that Door of Awareness does for the underprivileged is made possible thanks to the generous contributions made by people like you. Volunteers who want to go one step further organize charity campaigns and raise funds for Door of Awareness on a larger scale. These donations in any shape or form make a huge difference to our children and our organization. Volunteers keen on campaigning for us can organize drives or help by bringing their own unique ideas to the table.

Be a volunteer Teacher

Our most popular volunteer program is Teaching Internships at our schools. Teaching internships present an incredible opportunity for volunteers to gain experience and exposure, and more importantly, interact with our wonderful students. If you are a student and want to enrich your extra-curricular experience, or if you are a parent and you want a remarkable learning opportunity for your child, then a Teaching Internship at Door of Awareness may be the right choice for you.

Train our Teachers

In our quest to deliver the best possible educational services, we maintain a special emphasis on teacher training and development. We invite talented and qualified professionals to donate some of their valuable time to conduct workshops and seminars with our teachers, faculty, and students.

Join the Door of Awareness Youth Wing

Our Youth Wing consists of the most dedicated and capable volunteers from the finest schools and colleges in Lahore. The Youth Wing team is a diverse group of leaders that manage all volunteer-related work. This includes everything from Marketing and Publications to Activities and Events management. Being a volunteer at the Youth Wing is an amazing opportunity for you to gain invaluable leadership skills and develop an enduring spirit of teamwork, while at the same time making a positive difference to your community. Please get in touch with the management team if you want to get involved.

Organize Student Activities

At Door of Awareness, our aim is to make our schools fun and enjoyable for our students. In such a spirit, we are always looking for volunteers to organize fun and creative activities with our students and staff. Here, we highly encourage volunteers to be creative and enthusiastic. Previously organized activities include theatre performances, Fun Festivals, Birthday parties and more.

Any ideas? Get in touch!

If you have a novel idea or want to do something that’s not listed in our activities, please do not hesitate to contact us. We are always ready to accommodate fresh and innovative ideas, and welcome creative minds to join our cause. Please get in touch with our Head Office to share your idea and start something new!
